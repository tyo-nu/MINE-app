Metabolic In Silico Network Expansion Databases (v2.0)

Welcome to the home of MINE databases, version 2.0! MINE databases expand the known metabolome using the pickaxe reaction prediction framework. In MINE 2.0, we have rebuilt all MINE databases with a new set of reaction rules which cover significantly more biochemistry than the rules used in MINE 1.0. If it's your first time on the website, we recommend taking one of the tours below. Access to the database is also possible through a REST API.

Note: the Safari browser is not fully supported at this time.
