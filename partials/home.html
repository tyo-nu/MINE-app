Metabolic In Silico Network Expansion Databases

Welcome to the CD-MINE database. This database contains spontaneous reactions that occur under physiological conditions curated from literature as well as reactions predicted by generalized reaction rules. There are many ways to explore this data and if it's your first time on the website, we recommend taking one of the tours below.

General Website Tour

Learn how to use search the database with chemical identifiers or compound structures and explore data on MINE compounds and view computational predicted reactions.

Metabolomics Tour

Learn how to use search the database with mass/charge ratio data and explore and sort the resulting putative identifications.

Spontaneous Chemical Damage

Explore documented and proposed spontaneous chemical transformations of intracellular metabolites.
